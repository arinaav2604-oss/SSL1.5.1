Compliance Framework

Zero Tolerance for Statutory Deviation

Our compliance architecture protects both our institutional clients and every member of our deployed workforce. Every license, every certification, every statutory obligation — maintained without exception.

PSARA Licensed

Private Security Agencies Regulation Act licensing across 17 states — covering the full breadth of our pan-India operations.

ISO 9001:2015

Quality Management Systems certification governing our service delivery, documentation, and client SLA frameworks.
