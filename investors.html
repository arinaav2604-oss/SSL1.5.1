Material Risk Factors

Risk Disclosure & Mitigation

Investors are advised to carefully consider the following risk factors alongside Safecure's stated mitigation strategies before making investment decisions.

Regulatory Risk

PSARA licensing renewals are jurisdiction-specific and subject to state government discretion. Delays or non-renewals in key states could temporarily restrict operations.

Mitigation Strategy

Maintained clean compliance records across all 17 licensed states. Renewal processes initiated 6 months in advance.

Workforce Attrition

The security services industry faces relatively high attrition rates, which can impact service consistency and increase recruitment and training costs.

Mitigation Strategy

Structured career progression, statutory benefits compliance, and ex-force leadership improve workforce retention vs. industry average.

Client Concentration

A portion of revenues is concentrated among large banking and institutional clients. Loss of a major contract could materially impact short-term revenue.

Mitigation Strategy

Active diversification across sectors (Aviation, Hospitality, Corporate) reduces single-sector dependency.

Statutory Compliance Exposure

Non-compliance with labour laws (PF, ESIC, minimum wages) can result in penalties, litigation, and reputational damage.

Mitigation Strategy

Dedicated compliance team with zero-tolerance policy. Direct bank salary disbursement eliminates intermediary wage risk.

Technology Disruption

Increasing automation and AI-driven surveillance solutions may commoditize certain service offerings if technology adoption is delayed.

Mitigation Strategy

Ongoing investment in geo-mapping, attendance validation, and command centre technology infrastructure.
